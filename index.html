You get a full bike share system, with bicycle, high quality "lock-to" smart lock, user app and management software.

What can you do with our software?

Reserve a bike

Find bicycles at virtual hubs and reserve even before you are in the neighbourhood.

Unlock and ride

Use your smartphone's bluetooth connection to connect and unlock the chained wheel-lock.

Lock and Return

Lock your bike to infrastructure during your rental and return to a designated virtual hub when rental period ends.
